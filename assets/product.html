Generate OpenGraph images by layering colors, images, and text using your own templates.

Core integration

Override your default settings by editing your Joomla menu items, articles, or categories.

Use the power of custom fields

Want to use different images for your menu items, articles, and generated OpenGraph images? No problem! You can use Joomla! Fields to provide your custom images.

Stop wondering what the end result looks like. Preview it instantly from the template editor, with optional text debug information overlaid onto the preview.

Extensible
